## Annotation

| Operation		| Notation  		| Sentece representaion	|
|:-------------:|:-----------------:|:---------------------:|
| Negation		| $$\neg$$A ; A'	| not A				 	|
| Conjuncion	| A $$\land$$ B 	| A and B			 	|
| Disjunction	| A $$\lor$$ B  	| A or B			 	|
| Implication	| A $$\implies$$ B	| Impossible A without B|
| Equivalence	| A $$\iff$$ B		| If A, then B 			|
